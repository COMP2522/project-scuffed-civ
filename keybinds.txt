w - move camera up
a - move camera left
s - move camera down
d - move camera right
b - create building (worker to be selected)
m - create worker (maker) (requires building to be selected)
f - create soldier (fighter) (requires building to be selected)
c - collect (requires worker to be selected)
enter - next turn
space - reset camera
x - deselect entity
up arrow - zoom in
down arrow - zoom out
f3 - debug menu
